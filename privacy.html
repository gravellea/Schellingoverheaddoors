We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how Schelling Overhead Doors (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you visit our website https://www.schellingoverheaddoors.com (the “Site”), contact us, schedule a service, or otherwise interact with us.

1. Information We Collect
We may collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide directly including Name, phone number, email address, and home address (when you request a quote, book a service, or fill out our contact form). Any additional details you voluntarily provide (e.g., garage door photos, notes about your service needs)
  • Information Collected Automatically-IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system Pages visited, time spent on the Site, and referring website Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 6 – Cookies)
  • Information from Third Parties- Reviews you leave on Google, Facebook, or other review platforms, Publicly available information (e.g., to confirm your address for service)

2. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:

  • Schedule and perform garage door repair/installation services
  • Provide quotes and invoices
  • Process payments and issue refunds
  • Send service reminders, appointment confirmations, and follow-up messages
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax reporting, warranty records)
  • Detect and prevent fraud or safety issues

3. How We Share Your Information
We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only in these limited cases:
  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who help us operate our business (e.g., Clopay Corporation, payment processors like Stripe or Square, scheduling software, SMS/email providers like Twilio or Mailchimp, Google Workspace for internal records). These providers are contractually required to protect your data.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, subpoena, or government request.
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a sale, merger, or acquisition of our company.
4. Data Security
  • We use industry-standard measures (SSL encryption, secure payment gateways, restricted internal access) to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.
5. Data Retention
  • We keep your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes listed above or as required by law (typically 7 years for tax and warranty records).
6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies
  • Our Site uses cookies and similar technologies for essential functions (e.g., saving your quote form progress) and analytics (Google Analytics). You can control cookies through your browser settings. By continuing to use our Site, you consent to our use of cookies as described.
7. Your Choices & Rights (New York Residents)
You may:
  • Request to access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Opt out of marketing messages (text “STOP” to any SMS or click “unsubscribe” in emails)
  • Ask us not to share your data with third parties for marketing (we don’t do this anyway)
  • To exercise these rights, contact us at the details below.
8. Children’s Privacy
  • Our services are not directed to persons under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from persons under 18.
9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The new version will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.
